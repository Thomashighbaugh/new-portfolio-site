    Design Considerations!

    Digital Art Style: Art is a recent venture born of necessity in developing the Resurgence site that has picked up considerable steam since, as it happens I am much better at creating aesthetically pleasing pieces when I am not using my trembling hands to do so but instead a mouse and keyboard. This has lead me to explore the various approaches I have to my favorite subjects of my unusual style of photography. I attempt to show things to people as I see those them while retaining the perspective of human's being a small part of a bigger world by capturing as much of the sky and the image will allow for. The use of editing is not to enhance the picture but instead impart upon the viewer the feelings I felt when tasking it, which is easy to remember for me because the picture reminds me immediately of what I was feeling when I took it.

    Site UI/Ux: This particular combination of personalized artwork and deliberately simple to make it so hiring managers, or prospective clients, as easy an experience as possible considering the multitude of candidates they go over per day.The dark color pattern is to make the site easier to maintain in the night, as it does not cause strain on the eyes. Furthermore, the neon-black contrast employed was chosen deliberately to pull in visual cues from "cyberpunk" and especially "retrowave" both of which are stylistic paradigms associated with distinct types of electronic music (which I otherwise am not a fan of, but the aesthetics are beautiful and I am able to adapt them into my designs and art)

    The key to this pages design is to introduce some topics and cover some extraneous bits of pertinent information(like employment history and education) without creating a cluttered mess of paragraphs. This improves the lives of the customer by not ruining one's eyes from excessive brightness if seen at night (often when I have time to work on the project after working). This was achieved using this fancy card trick involving JS!
