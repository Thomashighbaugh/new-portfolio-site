    Design Considerations!

    Digital Art Style: All of the art I make, be it my branded clothing available through teespring, the digital pieces I blast Instagram with or the websites that I code, is all made with the particular vision of what I have learned is called the "Neon Noir" style. This means that it is a blend of vibrant colors with the dark "Film Noir"-like style characteristic of the eponymous film genre. Why this is my preferred style, I cannot say for certain as the whole of my artistic endeavors have spanned just these last few months and I go with what appeals to me personally. Its an aesthetic choice I suppose.

    Site UI/Ux: As discussed elsewhere, this site is designed with just a hint of the Neon Noir stylization that informs most of my creations, be they websites or some of the various pieces of art I design. The style is deliberately dark and cool, with the typography giving a sort of hip, electronic flare. As far as its functionality, it is designed to be both simple to use (press the card, read content,press close) and without burying the relevant information below the fold or within some cluttered mess typical of a new-to-tech portfolio site.
